Published by maxcooperative- Multi – Access Cooperative or MAX is a multi – purpose cooperative since 2008, managed by a team of professionals and self-employed workers with varied competencies in delivering hospitality service and human resource management including manpower outsourcing, we are socially responsible and flexible cost-efficient cooperative that provides solution of Contracting and Outsourcing Services.

Our Vision.

To be the most sought-after partner of the companies seeking a highly competent creative and well managed organization that provides very competent service contracting and outsourcing solution.

Our Mission.

Ensuring success of our client’s business operations by delivering innovative and unparalleled service that delight their customers. We sustain our partnership with our clients through a healthy and harmonious relationship.

    Housekeeping Package

    Housekeepers handle light cleaning needed for the upkeep of homes as well as commercial buildings, such as offices, hotels, and hospitals. Typically they'll replace bedding, clean rooms, halls and bathrooms, vacuum, sweep, mop, replace toiletries and make beds.

    Professional Laundry Services (AXS 360 and Lino 77, Inc)

    A laundry service is a service in a hotel that washes and irons clothes for guests. Almost all hotels have a laundry service. If you hand in clothes one day you should get them back a day or two later. The laundry service will wash, dry, and iron your clothes.

    Office Personnel

    Office personnel refers to the office staff that carries out all the administrative as well as clerical functions jointly to achieve the objectives of an organization. Office personnel refers to office chief, sectional chief, and assistant. All the functions of the organization are performed by office personnel.
